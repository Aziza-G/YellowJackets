ABOUT

Yellowjackets is an American coming-of-age survival drama series created and executive produced by Ashley Lyle and Bart Nickerson.
Equal parts survival epic, psychological horror story and coming-of-age drama,
Yellowjackets is the saga of a team of wildly talented high school girls soccer players who become the (un)lucky survivors of a plane crash deep in the remote northern wilderness.
The series chronicles their descent from a complicated but thriving team to savage clans, while also tracking the lives they’ve attempted to piece back together nearly 25 years later, proving that the past is never really past and what began out in the wilderness is far from over.

MAIN CHARACTERS

Jackie Taylor

Personality

Jackie Taylor is the charismatic, popular leader of the WHS Yellowjackets and Shauna's best friend.

As team captain and a popular student, she exudes grace and ease, making her a reassuring presence to those around her.
She is perhaps the most accurate representation of a typical suburban teenage girl, kind and loyal, yet at times self-absorbed and unaware of how her actions affect others.
Jackie values tradition, social structure, and the familiar rhythms of high school life.

Shauna Sadecki-Shipman

Personality

Shauna Shipman is intelligent, introspective, and emotionally repressed.
Despite her intelligence, Shauna has long struggled to express her emotions in healthy ways.

Taissa Turner

Personality

Taissa Turner was a standout player on the Wiskayok High School 'Yellowjackets' soccer team, known for her determination, intensity, and leadership on the field.
After the plane crash that left her and her teammates stranded in the wilderness,
Taissa emerged as one of the group’s most pragmatic and strategic voices, often advocating for rational solutions and action.
As a teenager, she exhibits a strong-willed determination and a relentless drive to succeed, often taking on leadership roles within the group.
